Zjrenju System introduction:

Zjrenju System has been under close-bata test since October 29th, 03 for about half a month. Bugs in the initial system were corrected, and RIF rule was introduced as well as both SAKATA and Two-Swap rules in order to promote the popularity of the new rule, and also to test the applicability of the new system which adopts new rules.
